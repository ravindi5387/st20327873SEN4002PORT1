About Us

Our Story

Family Budget Manager was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to help Sri Lankan families take control of their finances in a simple, intuitive way. We understand the unique financial challenges faced by families in Sri Lanka, and we've built a tool specifically designed to address those needs.

Our Mission

To empower Sri Lankan families with the tools they need to achieve financial stability and prosperity through better budget management, expense tracking, and financial planning.

Why Choose Us?

  • Designed specifically for Sri Lankan families and financial needs
  • User-friendly interface that works for all family members
  • Local currency (LKR) support with familiar categories
  • Secure and private - your data stays with you
  • Regular updates based on user feedback

Our Team

We're a small team of financial experts and software developers based in Colombo, passionate about helping families achieve financial wellness. Our diverse backgrounds in finance, technology, and user experience design allow us to create a product that truly meets the needs of Sri Lankan families.
